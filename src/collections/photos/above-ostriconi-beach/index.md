Ostriconi beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Corsica, at the entrance to the famous Agriate desert, known for the picture-perfect coastline.

The Agriates desert is a territory bordered to the south by a mountain range, the Serra di Tenda, and on all other sides by the Mediterranean Sea.

The beach can only be accessed by boat, or on foot from a footpath which leads off the main road, and from which you will have a breath-taking view.

The 800m of fine sand and white sand dunes make this place absolute paradise for holidaymakers and the locals of central Corsica.
